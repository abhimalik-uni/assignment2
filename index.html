The average number of properties sold per suburb across 2016 and 2017 was 43 properties. The sales difference represents is calculated by subtracting the average number of properties sold across all suburbs from the suburbs sales. Suburbs with sales greater than the average have a positive sales difference and are displayed in green whereas those with below average sales are displayed in red. Reservoir was the hottest suburb with 359 sales within the two years.
